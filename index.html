What I work on

Stick / arc welders

Shop, farm, and industrial transformer machines. Thyristor and silicon-controlled-rectifier control boards included — this is the deep end and I'm comfortable in it.

Engine-driven welder-generators

Gas and diesel welder-generators. Brushes, slip rings, exciters, control circuits, output problems, and the engine-to-generator handoff.

Not my table

Rates & the mobile question

Posted up front so there are no surprises on the phone.

  • Drop-off is strongly preferred. Bring the machine to me in northern Seguin and you get my full bench, my meters, and my full attention. This is where I can actually fix things.
  • Mobile visits are evaluation only. If I come to you, I can diagnose — I can't do a real repair out of a truck bed. Expect to haul it in afterward anyway.
  • Basic on-site evaluation: $200 for two hours, plus 48¢ per mile from northern Seguin, Texas.
  • You need to be close. I don't travel far for evaluations. If you're a long haul out, drop-off is your only realistic option.
  • Repair rates get worked out between us once I know what the machine actually needs. Every job is different and I won't pretend otherwise.
